# hop30 — "How it works" explainer script

A tight, no-BS voiceover (~60–75s). Visual cues in brackets.

---

**[Phone on a kitchen counter, someone clearing floor space]**

No gym. No equipment. No excuses. Just you, the floor, and about **35 minutes a day**.

**[App opens to "Today" — two circuits listed]**

Open the app. It tells you exactly what to do today — you never have to think about it.

**[Timer: big "30", a demo video looping]**

Every move runs **30 seconds**. You go at *your* best — full effort, your pace. Can't do a full push-up? Drop to your knees. Too easy? Add a clap. **The move scales to you** — beginner or beast.

**[Voice cue plays, bell rings, green rest screen]**

A voice counts you in — *"Ready… three… two… one"* — the bell rings, you go. Thirty seconds on, thirty seconds rest. Screen turns green, you breathe, and the next move's already up with a video showing exactly how.

**[Quick montage: rounds ticking, calendar checking off]**

Four rounds. Two short circuits. Done in **32 to 40 minutes** — and the calendar checks your day off automatically.

**[Check-in: first photo → latest photo side by side]**

Snap a full-body photo on day one. Each month, snap another — the app's AI compares them and tells you, honestly, **what's actually changing**. Progress you can see, not guess.

**[Person finishing, breathing hard, smiling]**

It works because it's simple enough to *actually do* — every day, at home, with nothing. Pair it with the eating plan, show up, and your body does the rest.

**[Logo]**

No BS. Press start.

---

## Good-to-know (optional add-ons / on-screen text)

- **Rest days are part of the plan** — recovery is where the results happen. Don't skip them.
- **Pick your goal:** Lean (shred) or Bulk (build) — same system, different calendar + diet.
- **Form over speed.** Quality reps beat rushed ones; modify down or up as needed.
- **Train with a friend** — add them in the app and check off both your calendars from one phone.
- **Music built in** — your own playlists, matched to each workout.
